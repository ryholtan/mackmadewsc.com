About Mack Made

At Mack Made, we forge timeless creations from wood and steel—each piece tells a story of rugged craftsmanship, precision, and passion. Inspired by the spirit of the bulldog, our work embodies grit and character.

From custom furniture to functional art, every project is a blend of strength and beauty.

Our Craftsmanship

We specialize in handcrafted wood and steel designs that stand the test of time. Whether it's a live-edge dining table, a metal wall piece, or a custom-built shelving system, we bring bold ideas to life.

Each creation is built with integrity, and no two pieces are alike.

Materials & Process

We source the finest hardwoods and premium steel, blending rustic warmth with industrial edge. Our process involves cutting, welding, sanding, and finishing—by hand—ensuring each piece is a true original.

Flames, sparks, and grit fuel our work—this is craftsmanship with character.
