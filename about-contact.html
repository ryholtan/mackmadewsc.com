Meet the Makers

Mom & Dad
Mom stains and finishes the wood while Dad handles the forge. Together, they started Mack Made with nothing but a welder and a dream.

Son
Leads design and custom orders. He’s the one making sparks fly (literally) in the back of the shop.

The Fam
Mack Made Wood & Steel Creations is a family-run shop in Southeast Idaho. Born from a garage, a grinder, and a dream, this small-town family business crafts each piece with fire, sweat, and pride.

Dad forges the steel. Mom shapes and finishes the wood. Their son leads creative design, while their daughter and her husband handle logistics, photography, and customer care. Every item tells a story—and you're part of it when you buy Mack Made.
